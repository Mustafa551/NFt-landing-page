THE PROJECT

Bad Panthers is an exclusive and unique collection of 10,000 Panthers who unite together on the Ethereum Blockchain. Each Bad Panther has its own unique DNA, they are algorithmically generated from over 150 traits.

Bad Panthers are stored as ERC-721 tokens on the Ethereum blockchain and hosted on an interplanetary file system (IPFS).

Entering into the Bad Panthers Community means joining a family full of investors and NFT enthusiasts who believe in the future of Cryptocurrencies and blockchain technology.
